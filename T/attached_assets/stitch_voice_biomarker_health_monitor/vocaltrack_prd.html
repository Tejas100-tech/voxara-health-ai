# Product Requirements: VocalTrack - AI Voice Diary ## Overview VocalTrack is a non-invasive, AI-powered health monitoring application that uses vocal biomarkers to track chronic conditions like Asthma, Parkinson's, and Depression between clinical visits. ## Core User Flow 1. **Daily Check-in:** User receives a notification to record their daily voice sample. 2. **Recording:** User speaks for 15 seconds (e.g., describing their day or reading a standard text). 3. **AI Analysis:** The system processes the audio for tremors, pitch, and breathlessness. 4. **Feedback:** User sees immediate insights and long-term trends. 5. **Alerts:** The system notifies the user or doctor if significant anomalies are detected. ## Key Features - **Voice Recording Module:** 15-second limit, noise reduction, and visualization. - **Biomarker Dashboard:** Visual representation of MFCC, pitch, and speech rate. - **Trend Analysis:** Graphs showing health stability or deterioration over weeks/months. - **Condition-Specific Insights:** Tailored feedback based on the patient's specific diagnosis (e.g., tremors for Parkinson's). ## Target Audience - Patients with chronic respiratory, neurological, or mental health conditions. - Healthcare providers monitoring patient progress remotely. - Pharmaceutical researchers tracking drug efficacy.
